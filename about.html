About Biosecurity-AI

AI technologies are advancing at an unprecedented rate. But so is misuse.

What we do

We provide specialist consulting at the intersection of microbiology and AI, focused on safeguarding against the misuse of large language models whilst ensuring the continued beneficial use of AI in the life sciences.

Our services include evaluation datasets, supporting with red teaming activities within the field of biosecurity, risk assessments of existing models, and strategic advice to ensure AI systems remain aligned with safe, research-driven, and beneficial applications of biological knowledge.
